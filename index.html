My Approach

Three quiet principles.

I don't do meal plans you can't sustain, or diets that make you hate your own kitchen. We work slowly, honestly, and with your life as the starting point.

01

Listen first

Your history, hormones, sleep, stress, workout pattern, childhood foods. We start with the full picture — not a calculator.

02

Small shifts

One sustainable change at a time. No elimination overnight. We build habits you'd keep even after we stop working together.

03

Track what matters

Not weight. Energy, digestion, cycles, mood, lab markers. The things that actually tell us if your body is thriving.

Hello — I'm Maya

A nutritionist who still makes dal every Tuesday.

I trained in clinical nutrition after my own gut health fell apart in my twenties — despite doing "everything right" by the internet's standards. The science helped me heal. But it was re-learning to cook the food my grandmother made that actually brought me home.

Today I work with women who are tired of being told their bodies are a problem to be fixed. Together we find a way of eating that feels both rigorous and tender — backed by labs, rooted in your kitchen.
